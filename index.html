How I Teach

My maker education process.

Step 01

💡

Spark & Ideate

Brief with a real problem. Design thinking to generate and filter ideas.

Step 02

✏️

Sketch & Plan

CAD sketching, circuit planning, BOM and fabrication plan.

Step 03

🔧

Build & Fabricate

3D print, laser cut, solder, code Arduino/RPi. Students do the making.

Step 04

🔬

Test & Iterate

Debug, improve, refine. Failure is a data point. Iterate until it works.

Step 05

🎯

Present & Scale

Demo day, documentation, next steps — class project to potential startup.
